KidTimer Privacy Policy

Helping families build healthier digital habits.

Last Updated: June 25, 2026

1. Introduction

KidTimer is a parental control and digital wellbeing application designed to help parents manage screen time and encourage healthier digital habits for children. This Privacy Policy explains what information is used, how it is used, and how it is protected.

2. Information We Collect

Depending on the features you use, KidTimer may collect:

  • Parent account information such as Google account name, email address, and profile picture (if available).
  • Child profile information entered by parents.
  • Application preferences and settings.
  • Screen time usage information required for parental control features.
  • Permission status required for app functionality.

3. Google Sign-In & Firebase Authentication

KidTimer uses Google Sign-In through Firebase Authentication to securely authenticate parents or guardians. The following information may be received from Google based on your account permissions:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Profile picture (if available)

This information is used only for authentication, account management, and providing application functionality. KidTimer does not sell your information to third parties.

4. Permissions We Use

  • Usage Access Permission: Used to measure application usage and calculate screen time.
  • Accessibility Service: Used to enforce parental controls and screen time restrictions.
  • Display Over Other Apps: Used to display reminders and lock screens.
  • Notification Permission: Used to provide alerts and reminders.
  • Foreground Service: Used to keep monitoring active.

5. How We Use Information

  • Authenticate parent accounts.
  • Create and manage child profiles.
  • Provide parental control features.
  • Calculate screen time.
  • Maintain application functionality.
  • Improve application performance and reliability.

6. Data Storage

KidTimer may securely store authentication-related information using Firebase services. Some application settings and preferences may also be stored locally on your device. We do not sell personal information to third parties.

7. Children's Privacy

KidTimer is intended to be configured and managed by parents or legal guardians. Children should only use the application under parental supervision. Parents are responsible for creating and managing child profiles.

8. Third-Party Services

KidTimer may use trusted third-party services including:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Google Sign-In

Third-party services have their own privacy policies.

9. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information used by KidTimer. However, no internet transmission or electronic storage system can guarantee absolute security.
